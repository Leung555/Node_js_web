Why we exist

Every car needs a Papa. Most of us don't have time to be one.

There used to be someone in the house who just took care of it — who knew when the oil was due, which mechanic to trust, and exactly how much a job should cost. You handed over the keys after school and stopped thinking about it.

Papapick is that person, as a service. We notice when your car is due, take it where it should go, stand over the job so nobody pads the bill, and bring it back clean — usually before you've finished your coffee.

We know the garage

Every workshop on our list has been checked out first. Your car only goes where we'd send our own.

We bargain like it's ours

We sit through the quote, question every line item, and push back on anything that smells like an upsell.

We carry the risk

Every trip is insured. If something goes wrong while your car is with us, it's on us — not on you.

How we stay accountable

Standards, not improvisation.

“100% safety” isn't a slogan here — it's a checklist, a contract, and a policy number.

Written standards

Every driver follows the same checklist, from pickup photos to garage sign-off. Nothing is left to memory.

In-house legal team

Contracts, claims, and disputes go through our own legal team — not a call-centre script.

Insured, every trip

Each pickup is covered. If the unforeseeable happens once, we take full responsibility — no arguments.

Vetted drivers

Every driver is background-checked and trained on our standard before they're handed a customer's keys.

How we work

From the reminder to the return drive, in seven steps.

This is the same loop for almost every client, twice a month: we notice, we book, we go get it, we bargain, and we bring it home.

01

We notice

Most cars need this twice a month. We track the schedule and remind you before the dashboard light does.

02

You book

Pick a date, time, and location on the website. Slots update in real time — no phone tag.

03

We pick up

A vetted driver collects your car at the agreed time, logs the handover, and takes condition photos.

04

We deliver it

Your car goes straight to a garage from our vetted list — or yours, if you'd rather.

05

We bargain

Our staff stays for the diagnosis and negotiates the quote before any work starts.

06

You approve

We send the agreed price to your LINE chat. Work only begins after you say go.

07

We bring it back

Your car is returned, on time, to the place you chose — with the receipt waiting in your chat.

Good to know

Most clients book us twice a month

That's roughly how often a daily-driven car needs a check-up or top-up. We'll remind you before each one is due, so booking becomes a habit rather than a chore.

Need something outside these hours — an early-morning office drop-off, say? Message us on LINE and we'll see what we can arrange.

How we charge

One flat fee. No surprises.

You only ever pay two things: the garage's actual invoice — which we negotiate down and show you before any work starts — and our flat pickup-and-return fee. No commission added on top of the garage's bill.

  • No commission added to the garage's invoice
  • No charge for status updates sent to your LINE
